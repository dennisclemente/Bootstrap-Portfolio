About Me

Web Producer + SEO Specialist + Writer

Based in New York, Dennis Clemente works as a Web Producer and Multicultural Copywriter. He has also been known to write for such publications as BBC Business Traveller and NBC Asian America. He currently works as an SEO specialist and content strategist for various companies, writing about global mobility, business travel and technology.

Although not a trained designer, he is certified in Adobe's Creative Suites--Photoshop, Illustrator and In-design with a year-long experience in Axure and UXPin for UX/UI design.
